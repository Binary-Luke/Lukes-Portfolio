About me

Hello World ! My name is Luke Filipe Sousa. I am currently in school at Coding Temple as a full-stack developer.
My mission is to create educational games for websites or apps that are focused on being user-friendly.

Coding and programming is fairly new to me, but I have experience working to develop more efficient systems for a business and I believe programming will allow me to take that three steps further.


I managed a diner in New Jersey for two and a half years directly after Covid-19 began. When the lockdowns began the business was forced to change and I spearheaded the business into to-go and delivery. I am proud to say my diner not only survived the lockdown, but thrived and grew from it. Before then, I attended Rutgers University of New Brunswick for two years. Although there were other issues, the main reason I had left was the amount of debt I would accrue in four years to leave without a skill. (My master would have been communications)


This, I believe, is partly the reason I know I will be an effective and productive programmer. It is the combination of learning problem solving, mixed with the discipline of a language. The feeling is tremendous when you put hours into something and the result is something real and interactive.


Since then I have moved down to Austin, Texas for a fresh start in a place loaded with oppurtunities. In Austin, I am working as a teacher in an after-school STEM program with kids.
